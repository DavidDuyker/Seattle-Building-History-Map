## Public market

Pike Place opened in **1907** as a city-sponsored farmers market so shoppers could buy produce direct from growers, cutting out price-gouging middlemen.

## Today

The nine-acre historic district mixes fishmongers, crafts, restaurants, and the Market’s social services—still one of the most-visited places in the city.
