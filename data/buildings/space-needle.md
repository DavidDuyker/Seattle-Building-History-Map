## Fair legacy

Built for the **1962 Century 21 Exposition**, the Space Needle helped define Seattle’s skyline and remains one of the most recognizable observation towers in the world.

## Design

The saucer-shaped “top house” and narrow waist were engineered for wind loads on the former fairgrounds, now Seattle Center.
